About IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. With 400,000+ members in 160+ countries, IEEE shapes the future of tech.

Visit IEEE.org

IEEE SB . LBSITW

The IEEE Student Branch at LBS Institute of Technology for Women is a vibrant community of future engineers committed to learning, collaborating, and growing together through innovative events.

IEEE Ignite

IEEE Ignite is our flagship annual event - a two-day celebration of technology, creativity, and professional development. We empower students to spark their potential and shape tomorrow's innovations.
